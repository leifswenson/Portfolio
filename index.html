About

Get to know me.

You: Who are you exactly?

Me: Well, I’m glad you asked. Let’s start with my name, my name is Leif. Want to know more?

You: YES, YES!

Me: Wonderful. Well, I was born and raised in Minnesota where I developed a love for the outdoors. It was because of that love for the outdoors that I majored in biology at the University of Minnesota Morris where I gained a deeper understanding of nature and biological systems. Through my study I found many parallels between biology and software. It is only when all pieces of a system are healthy that the whole can function properly. My approach to problem solving is informed by my background in biology. I have a great appreciation for the scientific method and believe that asking good questions will lead to great results.

You: That sounds really interesting.

Me: Thanks.

You: How can we work together?

Me: I am glad you asked. You can connect with me on LinkedIn or send me an email. I look forward to hearing from you.
